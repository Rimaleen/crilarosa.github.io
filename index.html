ABOUT ME

My name is Cristina La Rosa from Italy.

I’m a Graphic Designer and Digital Artist with a strong interest in visual communication and creative experimentation.

I enjoy working across different mediums, from graphic design and digital illustration to video, photo editing, and creative coding. I’m curious by nature and always motivated to learn new tools and techniques to expand my creative skills.

Beyond design, I love playing videogames, tabletop role-playing games like Dungeons & Dragons, and imagining characters and worlds for my own stories. These passions strongly influence my creative approach and inspire many of my visual projects.
